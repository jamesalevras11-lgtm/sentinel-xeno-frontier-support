Privacy Policy for Sentinel Xeno Frontier™

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Information collection

Sentinel Xeno Frontier™ does not require users to create an account. The game does not include third-party advertising or analytics services. Game progress, settings, preferences, and locally earned in-game items may be stored on the user's device. Removing the app or clearing its storage may remove locally stored progress.

Optional Google Play purchases

Sentinel Xeno Frontier™ may offer optional digital purchases through Google Play. Payment processing is handled by Google Play. The developer does not receive or store the user's full payment-card information. Google Play may process purchase-related information under Google's own privacy policies and terms.

Children's privacy

Sentinel Xeno Frontier™ is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security and changes

The developer does not operate a server that stores users' personal information for Sentinel Xeno Frontier™. This policy may be updated if the game's features or data practices change. Any update will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
